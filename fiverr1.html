FAQ

What makes you different from other Pro designers?

We have more than 10 years of experience working with high profile clients like Intel, Google, Mercedes, Emirates and Governments (Dubai and Abu Dhabi). We ensure user engagement in the projects to add their personal touch in the final product.

What is a revision?

A revision constitutes a change or variation to an initially presented—or in-progress—design concept and is typically a change of less than 1/4 of the design. A revision is simply meant to be a slight modification or course correction to progress toward the final design.

Do you understand English?

Yes, our communication handler is a native English speaker. So, communication won't be an issue as it goes "Communication is the KEY"

Do You Have Any Portfolio?

Yes, You can check some of my notable projects attached in the PDF section of this Gig. You can also check my Portfolio. https://www.fiverr.com/users/RoshanLeanage/portfolio?origin=seller_profile

What is your work flow?

Gather Requirements (Provided by you) -> Initial Concept -> Client Feedback -> Implementation of Required Changes (If any) (1st Revision) -> Client Feedback -> Implementation of Required Changes (If any) (2nd Revision) -> Client Feedback -> Final Delivery

Does these packages include mobile design?

No, for mobile design please contact us we'll make a custom offer for you.

Will you provide design conversion to HTML/CSS?

Yes, I provide HTML/CSS conversions of designs as well. But it will cost extra and you have to ask for a custom order for this.

Do you offer any other services?

Yes, as a matter of fact, we do, which includes, Branding (Includes everything related to branding), Conversion of design to HTML/CSS and graphic design.

What Project Model Do You Use In Your Projects?

I Use Agile, for maximum client interaction and satisfaction with the project.
